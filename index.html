How Our SMS Service Works

Opt-In Process

To receive Daily Wellness Check messages, you must explicitly opt-in using the form above. Here's how it works:

  • Fill out the sign-up form with your name and mobile phone number
  • Check the consent box agreeing to receive SMS messages
  • Click "Subscribe to Daily Wellness Checks"
  • You will receive a confirmation text message asking you to reply YES
  • Reply YES to activate your subscription (double opt-in for your protection)

Message Frequency: You will receive approximately 1-3 SMS messages per day, depending on your configured wellness check schedule.

Standard rates apply: Message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

Types of Messages You'll Receive

Messages are transactional and informational in nature, including:

  • Daily wellness check reminders (e.g., "Time for your daily wellness check! Reply YES when complete.")
  • Medication reminders (e.g., "Reminder: Take your morning medication")
  • Health tracking prompts (e.g., "Don't forget to log your water intake today")
  • Confirmation messages acknowledging your responses

How to Opt-Out

You may stop receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message (instant unsubscribe)
  • Emailing larry.lemmon.ll@gmail.com with your opt-out request
  • Calling (317)397-9578 to request removal

There are no fees to opt-out. Unsubscribing is immediate and free.
