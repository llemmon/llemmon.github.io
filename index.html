Welcome to Daily Wellness Check

Personal wellness reminder and notification service for individual health management.

About Our Service

Daily Wellness Check is a personal notification system that sends SMS reminders and wellness check-ins to help individuals maintain their health routines. This service is provided for personal use to support daily health tracking and medication reminders.

SMS Messaging & Opt-In Process

How You Consent to Receive Messages

Users provide explicit consent to receive SMS messages through one of the following methods:

  • Direct verbal consent via phone call where the user provides their phone number and confirms they wish to receive wellness check messages
  • Written consent via email requesting enrollment in the service
  • Explicit opt-in during initial setup where users provide their phone number specifically to receive daily wellness reminders

Message Frequency: Users will receive approximately 1-3 SMS messages per day, depending on their configured wellness check schedule.

Types of Messages You'll Receive

Messages are transactional and informational in nature, including:

  • Daily wellness check reminders (e.g., "Time for your daily wellness check! Reply YES when complete.")
  • Medication reminders (e.g., "Reminder: Take your morning medication")
  • Health tracking prompts (e.g., "Don't forget to log your water intake today")
  • Confirmation messages acknowledging your responses

How to Opt-Out

You may stop receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message
  • Emailing larry.lemmon.ll@gmail.com with your opt-out request
  • Calling (317)397-9578 to request removal

There are no fees to opt-out. Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply to SMS messages.
