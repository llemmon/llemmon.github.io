Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Daily Wellness Check ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our SMS notification service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our wellness reminder service:

  • Contact Information: Phone numbers and names of users who opt-in to receive SMS notifications
  • Message Data: Records of messages sent and responses received for service delivery purposes
  • Consent Records: Documentation of how and when you provided consent to receive messages

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To send wellness check reminders and health-related notifications via SMS
  • To process your responses to our messages
  • To maintain records required for compliance with telecommunications regulations
  • To honor opt-out requests and manage your communication preferences

We do not:

  • Sell your information to third parties
  • Share your information for marketing purposes
  • Use your data for any purpose beyond providing the wellness check service

SMS Messaging Terms

By opting in to our service, you agree to receive SMS messages related to wellness reminders. Messages are sent via toll-free number for:

  • Daily wellness check-in reminders
  • Medication and health routine notifications
  • Confirmations and service-related updates

Message frequency: Approximately 1-3 messages per day. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt-out at any time by replying STOP or contacting us directly.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Your phone number and personal information are stored securely and accessed only as needed to provide the service.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. If you opt-out of our service, we will retain only the minimum information required to honor your opt-out request and meet regulatory requirements.

Third-Party Service Providers

We use Twilio as our SMS messaging platform. Your phone number and message content are transmitted through Twilio's services to deliver messages to you. Twilio's use of your information is governed by their privacy policy and our agreement with them strictly limits how they can use your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out of receiving messages at any time
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing
